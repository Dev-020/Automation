<div class="ddbc-html-content"><h1 data-start="264" data-end="284"><strong data-start="266" data-end="284">Tandem Paragon</strong></h1>
<p data-start="391" data-end="525">You are trained to operate as a perfectly synchronized combatant, amplifying both offense and defense when fighting alongside an ally.</p>
<p data-start="527" data-end="711">You designate one willing creature you can see within 30 feet as your <strong data-start="597" data-end="615">Tandem Partner</strong> at the start of combat (no action required). You may change partners at the start of your turn.</p>
<hr data-start="713" data-end="716">
<h2 data-start="718" data-end="763"><strong data-start="721" data-end="763">I. Unified Pressure&nbsp;</strong></h2>
<p data-start="765" data-end="820">Once per turn, when you deal damage to a creature with:</p>
<ul data-start="821" data-end="901">
<li data-start="821" data-end="840">
<p data-start="823" data-end="840">A weapon attack</p>
</li>
<li data-start="841" data-end="859">
<p data-start="843" data-end="859">A spell attack</p>
</li>
<li data-start="860" data-end="901">
<p data-start="862" data-end="901">Or a spell that forces a saving throw</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-start="903" data-end="964">you can mark that creature until the start of your next turn.</p>
<p data-start="966" data-end="1089">If your Tandem Partner damages the marked creature before the mark ends, one of the following effects occurs (your choice):</p>
<ul data-start="1091" data-end="1311">
<li data-start="1091" data-end="1214">
<p data-start="1093" data-end="1214">The attack deals extra damage equal to <strong data-start="1132" data-end="1164">1d8 + your proficiency bonus</strong> (same damage type as the triggering attack), or</p>
</li>
<li data-start="1215" data-end="1311">
<p data-start="1217" data-end="1311">The target has disadvantage on the next saving throw it makes before the end of its next turn.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-start="1313" data-end="1389">If your Partner marks a creature first, you can trigger this effect instead.</p>
<hr data-start="1391" data-end="1394">
<h2 data-start="1396" data-end="1449"><strong data-start="1399" data-end="1449">II. Layered Defense&nbsp;</strong></h2>
<p data-start="1451" data-end="1526">While you are within 30 feet of your Tandem Partner and can see each other:</p>
<p data-start="1528" data-end="1550">When either of you is:</p>
<ul data-start="1551" data-end="1600">
<li data-start="1551" data-end="1575">
<p data-start="1553" data-end="1575">Hit by an attack, or</p>
</li>
<li data-start="1576" data-end="1600">
<p data-start="1578" data-end="1600">Fails a saving throw</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-start="1602" data-end="1644">the other can use their reaction to grant:</p>
<ul data-start="1646" data-end="1806">
<li data-start="1646" data-end="1691">
<p data-start="1648" data-end="1691">Resistance to the triggering damage, <strong data-start="1685" data-end="1691">or</strong></p>
</li>
<li data-start="1692" data-end="1806">
<p data-start="1694" data-end="1806">A bonus to the saving throw equal to the reactor’s proficiency bonus (potentially turning failure into success).</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-start="1808" data-end="1897">You can use this feature a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus per long rest.</p>
<hr data-start="1899" data-end="1902">
<h2 data-start="1904" data-end="1955"><strong data-start="1907" data-end="1955">III. Tactical Convergence</strong></h2>
<p data-start="1957" data-end="2029">If both you and your Tandem Partner target the same creature in a round:</p>
<ul data-start="2031" data-end="2360">
<li data-start="2031" data-end="2111">
<p data-start="2033" data-end="2111">That creature’s speed is reduced by 10 feet until the start of your next turn.</p>
</li>
<li data-start="2112" data-end="2202">
<p data-start="2114" data-end="2202">Opportunity attacks made by either of you against that creature are made with advantage.</p>
</li>
<li data-start="2203" data-end="2360">
<p data-start="2205" data-end="2360">If the creature attempts to teleport or move willingly out of both your ranges, one of you can make an opportunity attack against it (even if teleporting).</p>
</li>
</ul>
<hr data-start="2362" data-end="2365">
<h2 data-start="2367" data-end="2415"><strong data-start="2370" data-end="2415">IV. Finisher Protocol</strong></h2>
<p data-start="2417" data-end="2564">Once per long rest, when you and your Tandem Partner both deal damage to the same creature in the same round, you may trigger one of the following:</p>
<ul data-start="2566" data-end="2869">
<li data-start="2566" data-end="2691">
<p data-start="2568" data-end="2691">The creature is knocked prone (no save if Large or smaller; Huge+ gets a Strength save vs 8 + PB + relevant ability mod).</p>
</li>
<li data-start="2692" data-end="2801">
<p data-start="2694" data-end="2801">The creature becomes vulnerable to the next instance of damage it takes before the end of your next turn.</p>
</li>
<li data-start="2802" data-end="2869">
<p data-start="2804" data-end="2869">The creature must make a Constitution save or lose concentration.</p>
</li>
</ul></div>
